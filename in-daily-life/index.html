Integrate the Last Two Ayats of Surah Baqarah into Daily Life

Opening Note

For us Muslims integrating the Last 2 ayats of Surah Baqarah into our daily lives is both spiritually significant and supported by numerous hadiths. Our beloved Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) told us various times, about their virtues, highlighting their protective, forgiving, and beneficial qualities. To feel more connected to Allah and for a boost of spiritual power, here is how you can integrate the last 2 ayats of Surah Baqarah into your daily life;

Ways to Integrate the Last 2 Ayats of Surah Baqarah:

Here are some purposes you can recite the last 2 ayats for based on the essence and hadiths:

Reciting Them Every Night:

Many scholars interpret a famous hadith of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) as a form of safeguarding from harm and fulfilling one's needs for the night. According to Sahih Al-Bukhari and Muslim, the Prophet (ﷺ) said:

  • "Whoever recites the last two verses of Surah Al-Baqarah at night, they will suffice him." (Sahih Bukhari, Hadith 5009; Sahih Muslim, Hadith 807)

The recitation of these ayats before sleep brings spiritual protection and sufficiency to the reciter, In Sha'Allah and Allah knows best.

Incorporating Supplication into Daily Prayers

These last 2 ayats of Surah Baqarah are a source of comfort in our lives while going through harsh times and facing calamities. Reciting them or reflecting on their meanings can remind you that Allah's mercy prevails, and knowing that any difficulty is within your capacity makes it easy to go through.

The last ayat ends with a heartfelt dua, you can consider asking this dua after you perform your daily prayers, asking Allah for mercy and strength to overcome daily challenges.

Closing Note

These are some ways by which you can incorporate the supplication of the last two ayats in your daily routine.
